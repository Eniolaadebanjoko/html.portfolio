The Best Movies According to Eniola

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Jumanji

I love the adventure, the suspense, and the way it keeps you on edge from start to finish. It’s one of those movies that pulls you into a whole different world.A magical board game comes to life, releasing wild jungle dangers into the real world, and the only way to stop it is to finish the game.

Home Alone

I really enjoy the strong family theme, the playful chaos, and the hilarious moments that never get old no matter how many times I watch it. It’s fun, heartwarming, and full of clever scenes. A young boy is accidentally left behind when his family travels for Christmas, and he must defend his home from two clumsy burglars using creative and funny traps.

Good Luck Charlie

Because I love a good, lighthearted sitcom that always makes me laugh and feel relaxed. The characters are so relatable, and the humor feels natural and comforting. The show follows the Duncan family as they navigate everyday life, with the older sister creating video diaries to give advice to her younger sister, Charlie, as she grows up.
