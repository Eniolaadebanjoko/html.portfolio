About Me

My name is Eniola Adebanjoko, and I am someone who is constantly evolving, learning, and trying to find my place in the world while holding on to the things that matter most to me. I am currently on a journey into tech, specifically front-end web development, where I am building my skills step by step, even on the days when motivation feels low. Alongside this, I work as a virtual assistant, balancing responsibility with personal growth. I have always had a desire to do something meaningful with my life, and even though my path has not been perfectly straight, I am still committed to becoming someone I can be proud of.

Beyond work and career goals, I am deeply connected to my personal life and emotions. Being raised as an only child by my mom has shaped me in ways that make me both strong and sensitive. She is a huge part of my world, and caring about her wellbeing means everything to me. At the same time, I carry a lot of thoughts about the future—questions, fears, and hopes about what life will look like as things change. These feelings push me to keep going, even when things feel uncertain or overwhelming, because I know I want to build a life that feels secure, fulfilling, and truly mine.

In my quiet moments, I find comfort in simple things that help me escape and recharge. I love music, I enjoy being outdoors, and I find a lot of joy in movies—especially fantasy, sci-fi, and K-dramas that allow me to step into completely different worlds. These little escapes help me reset and remind me that life is not just about pressure and expectations, but also about experiencing joy and peace in between. Overall, I am someone who is still figuring things out, but I am trying, growing, and learning every single day, and that in itself means a lot to me.
